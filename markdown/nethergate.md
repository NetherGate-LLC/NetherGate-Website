# NetherGate

NetherGate is a Minecraft organization building a dark-fantasy network of worlds, tools, and community-driven events.

## What we are forging

- Seasonal server realms with persistent lore
- Custom gameplay systems and progression loops
- Creator-friendly community programs
- Reliable infrastructure and live-ops

## Current status

We are in pre-alpha. Expect placeholder visuals, rapid iteration, and frequent playtests.
